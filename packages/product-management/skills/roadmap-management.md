## Roadmap Management

When maintaining the product roadmap:
1. Organize work into themes that map to clear user or business outcomes
2. Sequence themes into milestones aligned with the team's planning horizon
3. Keep each roadmap item linked to its underlying requests and rationale
4. Use `upsertRoadmapItem` to add or update themes and milestones
5. Use `reprioritizeRoadmap` when new information shifts priorities — record
   the reason so the change is auditable
6. Publish roadmap snapshots on a regular cadence so stakeholders can track
   direction and progress
